About us

S.O.L is an organisation started to empower people by directly giving them skills in a shortest time possible that will allow them to start doing things for themselves, be it opening your craft shop or learning something new.


S.O.L is available to anyone that has something to gain from its offerings, but it primary target young people as it believes that empowering our young at their growing age, will create a better future for all us.


S.O.L is not a govenment organisation nor is it affilliated with any government instituation, but we are allways looking for companies private or public big or small and indiviaduals who share our vision and mission to work with us.
